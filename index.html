Project 02Health App

Human Battery Baby人体电池宝宝

Independent Development

Health Gamification Application · Developed by Hongzhi Lu健康游戏化应用 · 卢鸿志独立开发

Human Battery Baby transforms personal energy management into a playful battery-pet interface. It estimates daily energy capacity with BMR/TDEE logic and turns eating, exercise, sleep, and health guidance into interactive charging and discharging actions.人体电池宝宝将个人能量管理转化为电池宠物界面。软件基于 BMR/TDEE 逻辑估算每日能量池，并把饮食、运动、睡眠和健康建议转化为可互动的充放电体验。

TDEE Energy PoolModels daily energy capacity based on profile data and activity level.根据个人数据与活动水平估算每日能量容量。
Food ChargingConverts food intake into energy recharge events.将食物摄入转化为电量补充事件。
Exercise DrainRepresents activity as battery consumption using MET-style logic.用类似 MET 的逻辑把运动表现为电量消耗。
Health CompanionProvides playful health-management feedback through a pet interface.通过宠物界面提供游戏化健康管理反馈。
